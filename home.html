What is Open Secience Hardware?

Science hardware encompasses the tools and machinery we use for scientific endeacors( for exeamle: microscope,encironmental sensor). Open Science hardware refers to dcience hardwaware that is open source - or free to use,modify, study or distribute. by making science hardware open, more people can have access to the tools we use to do science.Open source hardware is hardware whose design desing is made publicly available so that anyone can study, modify, didtribute, make and sell the design or hardware based on that design.(Gathering for Open Science Hardware) )
